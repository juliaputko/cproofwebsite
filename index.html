The Canadian-Pacific Robotic Ocean Observing Facility is a CFI/BCKDF-funded project to build ocean observing capcacity off the British Columbia coast. A fleet of autonomous gliders, floats, and moorings will provide ocean scientists with long-term monitoring of the ocean at the small scales important to resolve upper ocean physical and biological properties.
